Common questions

Quick Answers.

Do I need to change my phone number?
No. You keep your existing mobile number. We set up call forwarding so missed calls automatically route to your Tradie Receptionist number. Your customers always call the same number they always have.
What carriers does this work with?
Telstra, Optus, Vodafone, and most AU mobile carriers. We send you the exact USSD code to activate forwarding on your specific carrier - takes about 30 seconds.
Can I turn it off when I want to answer calls myself?
Yes. You can activate and deactivate call forwarding any time using a simple code on your phone. We include instructions for your carrier.
What does the AI say when it answers?
It answers in your business name, collects the job details, and closes with a callback promise. It never books appointments or confirms times - just captures the lead and gets it to you.
How quickly will I be set up?
Most tradies are live within 24 hours of signing up. We handle the entire setup - you just activate forwarding on your phone when we tell you it's ready.
What if a caller hangs up early or doesn't leave details?
The system handles incomplete calls gracefully. If there's not enough information to create a lead, you still get a fallback WhatsApp alert so you know a call came in.
